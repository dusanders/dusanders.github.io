About Dustin

Hello, my name is Dustin Anderson and I a love to build things. I am attending my final year at the University of Wisconsin-Superior earning a Bachelor's degree in Computer Science. I am passionate about all things code, always tinkering with what I can do and taking every chance I can to learn something new. If you put me on the spot about what I am most passionate about; I would say anything embedded as I like the idea of building software that interacts with the physical world. All of my projects have been created with Windows or Linux operating systems and using a host of different development tools including; Visual Studio 2013, Eclipse, Android Studio, Code Composer Studio, and IAR Embedded Workbench.
